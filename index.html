ORIGINAL STORY

Before anything else, we are collectors. Collectors of gins, whiskies, curiosities and, above all, memories. We are well-travelled and have collected memories from around the globe.

It is our passion for memories that drives our passion for hospitality. Our collection is not just a group of buildings, but a series of living histories, each waiting for its next chapter to be written by you.
